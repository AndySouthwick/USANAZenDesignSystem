#Seamless

The goal is to create a seamless, future proof, congruent user experience across all of our assets

#How?

By providing our Creative Teams, IT, and Associates reusable code and design concepts that can be used across all platforms

Design Principles

  • Signal to Noise Ratio
  • Hicks Law
  • Affordance

Development Principles

  • Leave code better than you found it
  • Reuse as much as possible Components are our friends
  • Keep it simple

Development Dependencies

Please download the required SASS mixins and Variables to make it all work

We also require v4-bootstrap Lets avoid making things look bootstrappy ok!
